WHAT IS A WEBSITE

A WEBSITE is a collection of digital information accessible through the internet.

Its like a sort of online document that can contain text, images, videos, and other multimedia elements.

WEBSITE are used for various purposes, such as sharing information, presenting products or services, interacting with users through forms, or enabling real-time communication.

To access a website, a web browser like Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox is used, and the corresponding address or URL is entered. Webpages can be static, with fixed content, or dynamic, with updated or interactive content.
